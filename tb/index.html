The Terebinth Translation of the New Testament

An independent, syntax-forward English translation of the Greek New Testament by Timothy Noe, presented in parallel with the Greek so the structure of each rendering can be examined directly.

About this translation

The Terebinth Translation (TB) seeks to convey the underlying Greek syntax in English for New Testament scholars and students of biblical Greek. Each verse is shown beside its Greek source text; hovering or tapping a Greek word reveals its lemma and part of speech.

The text is maintained at AncientLibrary.org, which is the authoritative source; this reader mirrors a dated snapshot so it loads quickly and reliably. The Greek is the SBLGNT (Society of Biblical Literature Greek New Testament).
